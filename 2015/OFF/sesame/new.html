Take your Patient Engagement to the
next level

Dental Sesame and Ortho Sesame help your practice build a loyal patient community that keeps appointments, pays bills faster, and refers friends and family. Designed exclusively for dentistry, they give your practice powerful patient engagement solution that will delight both your patients and team.

Automated Appointment Reminders and Communication

Keeping a full, efficient schedule is essential to the success of your dental or orthodontic practice, and enables you to provide your patients with the one-on-one attention they deserve. With our automated appointment reminders, patients can customize how they receive appointment reminders and communication from your office with email, text messaging, and/or voicemail. They can also download a reminder for use in their digital calendar, significantly reducing no-show appointments. When missed appointments do occur, you’ll be able to notify patients of their missed appointment with an automatic reminder to reschedule.

Easy-to-Use Schedule

Our easy-to-use scheduling system allows you to review the day’s appointment schedule, including confirmations and cancellations, with a calendar interface designed with your front office staff in mind. You’ll also have the convenience of viewing a patient’s appointment and payment history, as well as their scheduled future appointments, on a single screen.

Automated Financial Reminders and Online Bill Pay

Send automatic email financial reminders with convenient online bill-pay options to reduce outstanding balances. Your patients can simply log into their account profile, and review and pay their bills online directly through your website. All online payments are protected in a secure server via SSL (Secure Sockets Layer) so your patients’ credit card information is encrypted the moment they enter it.

Patient Portal

In addition to the ability to pay bills and view appointments online, patients can also use our Patient Portal to access their online health history forms, view their diagnostic images and X-rays, and save and print ledger statements for their personal financial records. They can also refer a friend to your practice and provide post-appointment feedback about their visit, all from one easy-to-use dashboard.

Practice Communication Preferences

Send customized digital newsletters, group messages (email blasts), patient referral requests, satisfaction surveys, and even holiday and birthday greetings with one, easy-to-use system. Interacting with your dental or orthodontic patients outside of traditional office communication is a versatile way to create engagement.

HIPAA Compliant Case Collaboration

Collaborate with professionals in your network via Doctor Login, a secure and HIPAA-compliant online forum (compatible digital-imaging system required). Share diagnostic images, records, and patient history with dental specialists needed to complete multi-disciplinary cases.
